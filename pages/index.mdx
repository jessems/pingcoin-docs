# Pingcoin Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for Pingcoin — the open-source app that detects counterfeit gold and silver coins using acoustic analysis.

## What is Pingcoin?

Pingcoin is a modern implementation of the centuries-old "ping test." When you tap a genuine precious metal coin, it produces a distinctive ring determined by its material composition and geometry. Counterfeit coins made from different metals produce noticeably different sounds.

The app uses your smartphone's microphone to record the tap, performs Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) analysis to extract frequency content, and compares it against our database of authentic coin signatures. Results come in seconds.

## Why Use Pingcoin?

| Problem | Pingcoin Solution |
|---------|-------------------|
| Tungsten-filled fakes pass weight tests | Acoustic test detects tungsten's dead "thunk" |
| Professional testers cost $1,500+ | Smartphone app costs $29/year (or free with limits) |
| Visual inspection misses sophisticated fakes | Sound analysis reveals material composition |
| Uncertainty about coin purchases | Quick verification before you buy |
